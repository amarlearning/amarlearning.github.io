About Us

The hunger for glory has taken man to unimaginable heights through unthinkable hurdles. It is this hunger that has driven the perseverant and the adroit since time immemorial. Rarely does an ambitious amidst the best of the best. It’s even more improbable that the achiever is indisputably recognized and lauded with worthy praise and in the process enjoys the innate satisfaction of having proven himself amongst the titans of that field. This February will stand witness to the days of fresh ideation and euphoria. It assures of being nothing less than a revolution in cultural globe, aptly known as ABHIVYAKTI. Abhivyakti is the one of the most sought after college Extravaganzas in U.P. The relative isolation of Lucknow from other cities is one aspect that makes Abhivyakti so special to every one who attends the fiesta. It is this very distance that guards us from the mediocrity of the out side world and helps us in spawning a cultural calibration unlike any other. The 10000+ students community from across the subcontinent that swarms. Abhivyakti represents the most productive consumers age group of 18-25 years. This coupled with the fact that Abhivyakti runs for 3 days, gives the sponsors an effective undiluted publicity unlike other college festivals. Moreover, your brand becomes synonymous with the memories that participating teams carry back to home from the Abhivyakti experience.

Star Night

Mamta Sharma

Mamta Sharma is an Indian singer. She is best known as a playback singer in Hindi films, particularly for films like Dabangg and Yamla Pagla Deewana, and often sings in a husky voice.
15,000 heads banging, 60,000 watts of sound, a reverberating stage, one night and your chance to be in the midst of it all. With people from Bollywood coming to our college for star nights, this fest surely rocks. The list includes - Vinod Rathore, Euphoria, Shibani Kashyap, Tantriks’ Band, Mika, dance group Zeal, Amit Kumar & Sumit Kumar, Jassi, Suraj Jagan, Remo Fernandes, Mahalakshmi Iyer, Usha uthup, Kunal Ganjawala and to top them all this years attraction.
